Prototype Settings

Webhook

Connected to the fixed n8n endpoint for live monitoring and reflection. Local mode and manual endpoint editing were removed for a cleaner demo.

Memory Limits

Only the most recent 15 chat messages are sent for monitoring, and the most recent 10 main-chat messages plus 10 reflection turns are sent for reflection, to reduce token usage and cost.

Protection Logic

Requests are rate-limited and popup warnings use cooldown logic so repeated risky messages do not spam the user.

The reflection modal now starts with a short summary and one grounded prompt instead of many stacked blocks.
